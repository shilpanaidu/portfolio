About Me

I am a qualified Software Developer with a graduate degree in Computer Science Engineering and over 6 years of experience. All these years, I have been associated with multi-national companies like Pegasystems and Infosys Technologies Ltd. working on technologies like Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and PRPC. I have worked on large scale, multi-tier applications building innovative technology solutions to help clients achieve their business objectives. These experiences were full of challenges and I learnt a lot by learning and sharing.

I believe I have a lot to offer and great potential to progress further in my career while consistently adding value to the clients. I have gained most recent experience in web development from IRIS, a UK based cloud services provider.
